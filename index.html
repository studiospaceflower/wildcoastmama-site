About Wild Coast Mama

We chose the van over the mortgage. Then life got interesting.

We're building a van on the Cornish coast, expecting our first baby, and figuring out what a different kind of family life looks like. This blog is the honest version — the spray foam and the cream crackers and the moments that were hard and the ones that were completely worth it.

Tilly the golden retriever has not been consulted on any of the major decisions. She has opinions regardless.
